📜 Chatify Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Chatify collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to transparency and security, with **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)** at the core of our service.

1. Information We Collect

**Crucial Note on E2EE:** The content of your messages, voice calls, and attached files are **End-to-End Encrypted**. This means the content is protected by cryptographic keys that only you and the recipient possess. **Chatify cannot decrypt, read, or listen to your E2EE communications.**

A. Account Information (Collected)

When you register, we collect and store the following essential data:

  • Contact Data: Email address.
  • Profile Data: Username, Display Name, and Profile Photo URL/Reference.
  • Authentication Data: Hashed (one-way encrypted) version of your password.

B. Service Data (Metadata Collected)

To operate our service, we must collect non-encrypted metadata:

  • Communication Records: Sender and recipient IDs, and the timestamp of when a message was sent or received. We do not log the content or location.
  • Device & Usage Data: Device type (e.g., "Chrome Desktop"), IP address (used only for security and rate-limiting), and crash reports.
  • Status Updates: Whether a user is "Online" or when they were "Last Seen."

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information only for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Sending notifications, routing messages to the correct recipient, and displaying user profile information.
  • Security: Authenticating users, preventing fraud, and detecting malicious activity.
  • Communication: Sending service announcements, security alerts, and responding to support requests.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Consent: With your explicit consent (e.g., connecting a third-party service).
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental subpoena. We commit to reviewing all requests for metadata and to challenging vague or overly broad demands. Since we do not possess message content, we cannot provide it.
  • Protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Chatify, our users, or the public.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and transfer your data. You can exercise these rights primarily through the Settings menu in the Chatify app. For formal requests, please contact us:

Email: privacy@chatify.com

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy within the app or on our website.
