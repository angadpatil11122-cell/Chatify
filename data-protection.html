🛡️ Chatify Data Protection Commitment

This document highlights Chatify's foundational commitments to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data, reinforcing the principles laid out in our full Privacy Policy.

Our core commitment is **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)**. This cryptographic technology ensures that the content of your messages, calls, and files are only accessible to you and the recipient. **Chatify has no way to read or access your private communication content.**

1. Data Minimization and Collection

We believe in collecting only the data absolutely necessary to provide a reliable E2EE service:

  • Minimal Metadata: We only collect essential metadata, such as account usernames and message timestamps, which are necessary for routing and notification purposes.
  • No Content Logging: We do not store, log, or record the actual content of your E2EE communications.

2. Security Measures in Place

We employ technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction:

  • Encryption at Rest and in Transit: All data stored on our servers is protected, and all connections between your device and our servers are secured using **TLS 1.3**.
  • Access Controls: Access to unencrypted metadata is strictly controlled and limited to authorized personnel who require it for specific operational tasks.
  • Hashed Passwords: User passwords are never stored in plain text but are secured using industry-standard hashing algorithms.

3. Your Data Rights (GDPR / CCPA Principle)

You maintain full control over your personal information stored by Chatify. This includes the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the metadata we hold about your account.
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate profile information directly via the app settings.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Delete your account and all associated metadata.
  • Portability: Receive your metadata in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

For detailed information, including legal grounds for processing and data retention periods, please refer to our comprehensive Privacy Policy.
