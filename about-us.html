👋 About Chatify

Our Mission: Private Communication is a Right.

Chatify was founded on the principle that your conversations should belong only to you. In an age of mass surveillance and data harvesting, we provide a reliable, simple, and impenetrable platform for secure communication.

Our Story: Building Trust, One Encrypted Message at a Time

We started Chatify in **[Insert Year]** with a small team of developers and privacy advocates who were tired of applications that claimed privacy but stored user keys. Our goal was to build a messaging app where the company itself could not access the contents of its users' messages. That commitment led us to **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)**, which is integrated into every message, call, and file transfer on our platform.

Core Values

These values guide every decision we make:

  • Privacy First: We adhere to strict data minimization principles. If we don't need the data to operate the service, we don't collect it.
  • Transparency: Our code, where possible, is auditable, and our policies (Privacy, Terms, Security) are written in plain language.
  • Security by Default: E2EE is standard for all communication. There are no "opt-in" privacy settings—it's just how Chatify works.
  • User Focus: We exist to serve the communication needs of our users, not the interests of advertisers or data brokers.
