⚠️ Chatify Responsible Disclosure Policy

At **Chatify**, we value the work done by security researchers in improving the security of our services. We are committed to working with the community to address security issues responsibly.

1. How to Report a Vulnerability

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in Chatify, please report it to us immediately and confidentially via our dedicated security channel:

Email your detailed report to:

security@chatify.com

Please include a detailed description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce it, and any necessary proof-of-concept code. We recommend using **PGP encryption** if the details of the vulnerability are highly sensitive.

2. Our Guidelines and Expectations

We ask that researchers adhere to the following principles:

  • **Do not** publicly disclose the vulnerability details (including on social media or public forums) until we have acknowledged the issue and had a reasonable time to fix it (typically 90 days).
  • **Do not** access, modify, or destroy user data. Always use test accounts for your research.
  • **Do not** engage in any activity that affects the stability of our service (e.g., Denial of Service, brute force attacks).
  • **Limit** the use of automated vulnerability scanners.

3. Our Commitment to Researchers

When you comply with this policy, we commit to:

  • Providing prompt acknowledgment of your report within **3 business days**.
  • Working quickly to validate and remediate the vulnerability.
  • Expressing our gratitude, which may include public recognition on our security page (if desired) and a small reward or bounty, where applicable.

Thank you for helping keep Chatify secure for everyone.
